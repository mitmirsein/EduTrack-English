# [레벨테스트 리소스] 중등 영어 레벨 테스트 - 중급 종합 (Intermediate)

- **평가 대상**: 중2 재원생 및 예비 중3 신원생
- **시험 시간**: 40분
- **총 배점**: 100점 (40문항, 각 2.5점)

---

## I. 문법 및 어법 (Grammar & Usage) - [100점, 각 2.5점]
**[1-2] 문장 변환 및 4형식 문형에 관한 질문에 알맞은 답을 고르세요.**

1. 의미가 같도록 바르게 전환한 것은?
   > He can give you good advice.
   ① He can give good advice you.
   ② He can give good advice to you.
   ③ He can give good advice of you.
   ④ He can give good advice for you.
   ⑤ He can give good advice about you.
   *정답: ② (수여동사 give + 직접목적어 + to + 간접목적어 구조)*

2. 다음과 의미가 비슷한 것은?
   > It can give you happiness.
   ① It can give happiness to you.
   ② It can give happiness for you.
   ③ It can give happiness of you.
   ④ It can give you to happiness.
   ⑤ It can give you for happiness.
   *정답: ① (수여동사 give + 직접목적어 + to + 간접목적어 구조)*

**[3-5] 미래 및 진행 시제 관련 질문에 답하세요.**

3. 나머지와 나타내는 시간이 다른 것은?
   ① We will do our homework.
   ② We won the game yesterday.
   ③ We're going to have a party.
   ④ We are going to go on a picnic.
   ⑤ My father is leaving tomorrow morning.
   *정답: ② (①, ③, ④, ⑤는 모두 미래 시간을 나타내나, ②는 명백한 과거 'yesterday' 사건)*

4. 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 어색한 것은?
   My father is ________ next Sunday.
   ① going to Seoul
   ② coming home
   ③ visiting our city
   ④ leaving for London
   ⑤ reading a newspaper
   *정답: ⑤ (가까운 미래 일정을 나타내는 현재진행형 구조로 쓰기에 ⑤는 어색함)*

5. <보기>의 밑줄 친 부분과 의미가 다른 것은?
   > <보기> We're <u>having</u> Mina's birthday party.
   ① He's coming soon.
   ② What are you thinking?
   ③ When is the class opening?
   ④ Where are going this afternoon?
   ⑤ Mike is going to Seoul tomorrow.
   *정답: ② (보기와 ①, ③, ④, ⑤는 현재진행형이 예정된 가까운 미래를 나타내나, ②는 현재 진행 중인 동작)*

**[6-10] 관계대명사 및 현재완료 시제에 답하세요.**

6. 다음 중 어법상 어색한 것은?
   ① Everything I told you was true.
   ② Suji is wearing a jacket that I like.
   ③ The people we met were very nice.
   ④ This is the watch whom I lost yesterday.
   ⑤ He uses the vegetables he grows in his farm.
   *정답: ④ (선행사 watch가 사물이므로 관계대명사 whom 대신 which 또는 that을 써야 함)*

7. 밑줄 친 부분 중 쓰임이 나머지 넷과 다른 것은?
   ① Is he <u>reading</u> a book?
   ② She is <u>wearing</u> a skirt.
   ③ I am <u>drawing</u> a picture.
   ④ They are <u>sitting</u> by the window.
   ⑤ Who is <u>coming</u> to the surprise party?
   *정답: ⑤ (①~④는 평서문/의문문 진행형이나, ⑤는 주어 역할을 겸하는 의문사 Who가 주어가 되는 진행 의문문)*

8. 빈칸에 들어가기에 어색한 것은?
   We were reading a book ________.
   ① then
   ② at that time
   ③ at two o’clock
   ④ next Sunday
   ⑤ while you were out
   *정답: ④ (과거진행형 시제와 미래 부사 next Sunday는 호응할 수 없음)*

9. 어법상 옳은 문장은?
   ① Jenny is cleaning her room yesterday.
   ② I'm going to watch a baseball game last Sunday.
   ③ They went to Seoul Grand Park this weekend.
   ④ I'm going to visit my aunt for summer vacation.
   ⑤ They have lots of snow yesterday in Canberra.
   *정답: ④ (①, ②, ③, ⑤는 시점 부사와 시제가 불일치함)*

10. 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
    - My uncle had a car accident last Saturday.
    - He ________ in the hospital since last Saturday.
    ① is
    ② was
    ③ will be
    ④ has been
    ⑤ have been
    *정답: ④ (since로 인해 과거부터 지금까지 지속되는 상태인 현재완료 has been)*

**[11-14] 현재완료 용법 구분에 답하세요.**

11. 밑줄 친 부분과 쓰임(용법)이 같은 것은?
    - He has been away from home for one year.
    ① She has lost her money.
    ② He has gone to the station.
    ③ I have seen the movie once.
    ④ I have known him since he was born.
    ⑤ I have already finished sending invitations.
    *정답: ④ (제시문과 ④는 since/for와 함께 쓰인 현재완료 '계속' 용법)*

12. 우리말과 뜻이 같도록 할 때 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
    "N세대에 대해 들어 본 적이 있니?"
    = Have you ever ________ the N Generation?
    ① hear of
    ② was heard
    ③ heard of
    ④ listened to
    ⑤ listen for
    *정답: ③ (현재완료 경험 구문이므로 hear of의 과거분사형인 heard of)*

13. 밑줄 친 부분의 용법이 보기와 같은 것은?
    > <보기> I have never <u>been</u> to Paris.
    ① He has <u>gone</u> to Venice.
    ② She has just <u>finished</u> the work.
    ③ Have you ever <u>heard</u> Westlife’s songs?
    ④ She has <u>lived</u> in the house for 10 years.
    ⑤ I have <u>known</u> her since I was 5 years old.
    *정답: ③ (보기와 ③은 현재완료의 '경험' 용법)*

14. 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
    She ________ sick since last Friday. She is in the hospital now.
    ① is
    ② were
    ③ been
    ④ has been
    ⑤ have been
    *정답: ④ (since로 인해 과거부터 지금까지 지속되는 상태인 현재완료 3인칭 단수형 has been)*

**[15-17] 지각동사 및 현재완료 응용문제에 답하세요.**

15. 밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 어색한 것은?
    ① He <u>smelled</u> something nice.
    ② I have never <u>saw</u> a rainbow.
    ③ I saw her <u>talk</u> with her friends.
    ④ There <u>were</u> a few people in the church.

    *정답: ② (have never 뒤에는 과거분사(p.p.) 형태인 seen을 써야 함)*

16. 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
    Somebody ________ my bag. I can’t find it.
    ① taken
    ② taking
    ③ was taken
    ④ has taken
    ⑤ have taken
    *정답: ④ (가방을 잃어버려서 지금 가방이 없는 상태(결과 용법), 주어 Somebody는 3인칭 단수)*

17. 대화의 빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
    - A: ________
    - B: No, I haven't.
    ① Are you a good singer?
    ② Do you have a computer?
    ③ Who has your pens now?
    ④ Did you have your lunch?
    ⑤ Have you ever been there?

**[18-22] 동사 변화표 및 현재완료 부정/의문 변환에 답하세요.**

18. 다음 동사의 변화형이 잘못된 것은?
    ① fall - fell - fell
    ② see - saw - seen
    ③ come - came - come
    ④ hear - heard - heard
    ⑤ climb - climbed - climbed
    *정답: ① (fall의 과거분사형은 fallen)*

19. 다음을 의문문으로 바르게 고친 것은?
    > She has finished reading the novel.
    ① Does she have finished reading the novel?
    ② Has she finished reading the novel?
    ③ Did she finish reading the novel?
    ④ Have she finished reading the novel?
    ⑤ Is she finished reading the novel?
    *정답: ② (현재완료 의문문은 Has + 주어 + 과거분사 구조)*

20. 밑줄 친 부분의 형태가 바른 것은?
    ① I have just <u>fell</u> off the ladder.
    ② Have you ever <u>heard</u> about the story?
    ③ She <u>has playing</u> the piano for one hour.
    ④ How long <u>have you study</u> English?
    ⑤ I have never <u>to see</u> her before.
    *정답: ② (①은 fallen, ③은 has been playing, ④는 have you studied, ⑤는 to가 빠진 see)*

21. 다음 문장을 부정문으로 바르게 고친 것은?
    > I have finished my homework.
    ① I didn’t have finished my homework.
    ② I can’t have finished my homework.
    ③ I am not have finished my homework.
    ④ I have not finished my homework.
    ⑤ I have finished not my homework.
    *정답: ④ (현재완료 부정문은 have/has + not + 과거분사 구조)*

22. 다음 문장을 ( )의 지시사항대로 고칠 때 바르지 못한 것은?
    ① Sumi helps her mother. (현재진행형) → Sumi is helping her mother.
    ② She has finished reading the novel. (의문문) → Has she finished reading the novel?
    ③ Sumi helps her mother. (현재진행형의 의문문) → Is Sumi helping her mother?
    ④ Ms. Song has been to the Netherlands. (부정문) → Ms. Song has been not to the Netherlands.
    ⑤ Sumi helps her mother. (현재진행형의 부정문) → Sumi is not helping her mother.
    *정답: ④ (현재완료 부정형은 has not been to... 여야 함)*

**[23-25] 현재완료 시제 불일치 및 예외 사항에 답하세요.**

    I ________ here for two years.
    ① lives
    ② living
    ③ have living
    ④ have lived
    ⑤ have been lived

24. 밑줄 친 부분의 표현이 적절하지 않은 것은?

    ④ I’ve <u>never seen</u> her.
    ⑤ He’s <u>just gone</u> out.
    *정답: ① (특정 과거 연도 in 1990은 현재완료와 쓸 수 없으며, since 1990으로 고치거나 과거동사 knew를 써야 함)*

25. 어법상 어색한 것은?
    ① Somebody has taken my bag, I can't find it.
    ② I have never seen a rainbow.
    ③ We have lived here for six months.
    ④ Julie has just broken a vase.

    *정답: ⑤ (과거시점 부사 last night은 현재완료와 결합 불가하며, 단순과거 lost를 써야 함)*

**[26-30] 조동사의 의미적 구분 및 동의어에 답하세요.**

26. 빈칸에 들어갈 말이 나머지와 다른 것은?
    ① ________ you busy?
    ② ________ these your books?
    ③ ________ your parents okay?
    ④ ________ your children at school?
    ⑤ ________ your friend Jane American?
    *정답: ⑤ (①~④는 주어가 복수/2인칭이므로 Are가 들어가나, ⑤는 주어 Jane이 3인칭 단수이므로 Is)*

27. 빈칸에 공통으로 알맞은 것은?
    - He will not ________ see from now on.
    - Will you ________ come to our party?
    ① can
    ② could
    ③ may
    ④ make
    ⑤ be able to
    *정답: ⑤ (조동사 will 뒤에는 또 다른 조동사인 can이 올 수 없으므로 be able to가 적절)*

28. 밑줄 친 부분의 쓰임이 나머지 넷과 다른 것은?
    ① You <u>must</u> tell the truth.
    ② She <u>must</u> be a clever girl.
    ③ I <u>must</u> clean my own room.
    ④ I <u>must</u> be at my office by nine.
    ⑤ That boy <u>must</u> practice the piano every day.
    *정답: ② (①, ③, ④, ⑤는 '해야만 한다(의무)'이나, ②는 '~임에 틀림없다(강한 추측)')*

29. 보기의 밑줄 친 부분과 의미가 같은 것은?
    > <보기> You <u>may</u> think I'm lonely.
    ① <u>May</u> you succeed!
    ② <u>May</u> I enter the room?
    ③ <u>May</u> I park the car here?
    ④ You <u>may</u> use my computer.
    ⑤ The news <u>may</u> not be true.
    *정답: ⑤ (보기와 ⑤는 '~일지도 모른다(추측)', ①은 기원, ②~④는 허가/허락)*

30. 밑줄 친 부분과 바꾸어 쓸 수 있는 것은?
    > You<u>’re supposed to</u> teach me how to use the computer.
    ① can
    ② must
    ③ may
    ④ do
    ⑤ need not
    *정답: ② (be supposed to는 '~하기로 되어 있다, ~해야 한다'의 의무를 뜻하므로 must와 바꿀 수 있음)*

**[31-34] 제안/요구/명령 동사의 that절 규정 및 부가의문문에 답하세요.**

31. 빈칸에 들어가기에 어색한 것은?
    He ________ that we take part in the meeting.
    ① suggested
    ② refused
    ③ insisted
    ④ demanded
    ⑤ proposed
    *정답: ② (제안/요청 동사 뒤의 that절 (should) 동사원형 규칙에서 refused는 이에 해당하지 않음)*

32. 두 문장의 뜻이 서로 다른 것은?
    ① Tom suggested my selling the house. = Tom suggested that I sold the house.
    ② He ordered Jim to go. = He ordered that Jim should go.
    ③ He agreed to divide the prize between Tom and Ann. = He agreed that Tom and Ann share the prize.
    ④ He recommended me to buy new ties. = He recommended that I should buy new ties.
    ⑤ She insisted on going there. = She insisted that she should go there.
    *정답: ① (suggested 뒤의 that절 동사는 동사원형 sell 또는 should sell 이어야 함)*

33. 밑줄 친 부분 중 생략할 수 있는 것은?
    I suggested that we <u>should</u> collect these things next week.
    ① suggested
    ② that
    ③ should
    ④ collect
    ⑤ next
    *정답: ③ (제안 동사 뒤의 that절에서 should는 생략 가능)*

34. 두 문장이 잘못 연결된 것은?
    ① You have to go there. = You must go there.
    ② He was able to find his lost bag. = He could find his lost bag.
    ③ He broke his leg, and he isn't able to walk. = He broke his leg, and he cannot walk.
    ④ Can you see the faraway stars? = Are you able to see the faraway stars?
    ⑤ Tell me what to do. = Tell me what should I do.
    *정답: ⑤ (what to do는 명사절로 바꿀 때 간접의문문 어순인 what I should do로 써야 함)*

**[35-40] 조동사 had better 및 가주어/동의 조동사 종합 문제에 답하세요.**

35. 다음 중 어법상 어색한 것은?
    ① I'm too busy to go out.
    ② I heard the rain fall on the roof.
    ③ You don't have to cry any more.
    ④ You had not better go to the PC room.
    ⑤ I will go to the zoo if it is fine tomorrow.
    *정답: ④ (had better의 부정형은 had better not이므로 had better not go로 써야 함)*

36. 다음 중 어법상 옳은 문장은?
    ① Inho has better get up early.
    ② You had not better watch TV.
    ③ He has not better swim alone.
    ④ Kate had better go and lie down.
    ⑤ You don't have better play with your brother.
    *정답: ④ (had better + 동사원형 규칙이 바르게 지켜진 문장)*

37. 다음 중 의미가 다른 하나를 고르시오.
    ① You must be quiet.
    ② You had better be quiet.
    ③ You should be quiet.
    ④ You ought to be quiet.
    ⑤ You have to be quiet.
    *정답: ② (had better는 충고/강한 경고를 나타내며, 나머지는 객관적 의무 '해야만 한다'를 뜻함)*

38. 다음 대화의 빈칸에 알맞은 말은?
    - A: I broke my leg.
    - B: You had ________ go to the hospital.
    ① do
    ② better
    ③ good
    ④ right
    ⑤ sorry
    *정답: ② (강한 충고 had better)*

39. 밑줄 친 부분 중 어색한 것은?
    ① You <u>have to be</u> home by six o’clock.
    ② I <u>have to go</u> home now.
    ③ The knife is sharp, so you <u>have to</u> careful.
    ④ She <u>had to wait</u> for an important phone call then.
    ⑤ We will be late, so we <u>have to run</u>.
    *정답: ③ (형용사 careful 앞에는 be동사가 필요함: have to be careful)*

40. 밑줄 친 부분과 바꿔 쓸 수 있는 것은?
    > You <u>don’t have to</u> worry about it any more.
    ① never
    ② need not
    *정답: ② (don't have to = need not = don't need to)*
